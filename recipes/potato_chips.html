Potato Chips

Home

A healthy alternative to regular store-bought potato chips!

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes
  • olive oil
  • Salt
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat 2 baking sheets in a 425 degrees F oven.
  2. Toss thinly sliced russet potatoes with olive oil and salt.
  3. Spread on the hot baking sheets, sprinkle with sesame seeds and bake 10 minutes.
